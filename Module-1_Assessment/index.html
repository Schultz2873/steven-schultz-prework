About Me

Hello! My name is Steven Schultz. I'm 24 years old and currently live in Homewood, Illinois. I grew up on the South Side suburbs of Chicago and went to Saint Laurence High School. While at Saint Laurence I was a member of the National Honor Society and also played baseball and football. After graduating from Saint Laurence I continued my football career over at Benedictine University where I majored in Computer Science.

While I attended Benedictine I juggled two jobs along with being a full time student. One as a Life Cafe Supervisor at Lifetime Fitness and another as a DJ for Impulse Entertainment. I got into DJing when I was around 17 and it has been a huge passion of mine ever since.

After I graduated college and became a washed up collegiate athlete I've been spending my free time traveling, playing 16inch softball and rugby during the summer, and working on side projects. One I've been working on building a web application for my DJ company that sends out automated text messages and emails to our clients. The other is a mobile application called Spot Me, which in short, is a social media platform that will allow users to find fitness classes or trainers that interest them and it allows trainers and fitness instructors to promote themselves and their classes.
